Your Skin Type Results

Your skin type is: {{ skin_type|capitalize }}

{% if skin_type == 'dry' %}

Dry skin produces less sebum than normal skin and may lack the lipids needed to retain moisture and build a protective shield against external influences. This skin type feels tight and may look flaky, especially after cleansing.

Skincare Tips for Dry Skin:

  • Use gentle, hydrating cleansers
  • Look for products with hyaluronic acid, glycerin, and ceramides
  • Apply moisturizer while skin is still damp
  • Consider using facial oils
  • Avoid hot water when washing your face
{% elif skin_type == 'oily' %}

Oily skin produces excess sebum, giving the skin a shiny appearance. This skin type is prone to enlarged pores, blackheads, and acne. However, oily skin tends to age better with fewer wrinkles.

Skincare Tips for Oily Skin:

  • Use gentle foaming cleansers
  • Look for products with salicylic acid, niacinamide, and tea tree oil
  • Use lightweight, oil-free moisturizers
  • Don't skip moisturizer - it's essential for all skin types
  • Consider using clay masks 1-2 times per week
{% elif skin_type == 'combination' %}

Combination skin features areas that are dry as well as oily - typically with an oily T-zone (forehead, nose, and chin) and dry or normal cheeks. This skin type requires balanced care to address both concerns.

Skincare Tips for Combination Skin:

  • Use gentle, balanced cleansers
  • Consider multi-masking (different masks for different areas)
  • Look for products with niacinamide and hyaluronic acid
  • Use lightweight moisturizers all over, with richer products on dry areas
  • Exfoliate regularly but gently
{% elif skin_type == 'sensitive' %}

Sensitive skin reacts easily to products or environmental factors with redness, itching, or burning. This skin type requires gentle care and products free from common irritants.

Skincare Tips for Sensitive Skin:

  • Use fragrance-free, hypoallergenic products
  • Look for soothing ingredients like aloe vera, chamomile, and oatmeal
  • Patch test new products before full application
  • Avoid products with alcohol, fragrance, and essential oils
  • Use physical sunscreens rather than chemical ones
{% else %}

Normal skin is well-balanced - neither too oily nor too dry. This skin type has good circulation, a smooth texture, small pores, and is less prone to sensitivity or blemishes.

Skincare Tips for Normal Skin:

  • Use gentle cleansers to maintain your skin's natural balance
  • Look for products with antioxidants and peptides
  • Use a balanced moisturizer
  • Don't forget sunscreen daily
  • Focus on maintaining your skin's health
{% endif %}
